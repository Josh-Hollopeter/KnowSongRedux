KnowSong

Free Trivia

KnowSong provides a way for people to test their knowledge of music artists. Connect a Spotify account and quickly start a trivia game! Our game modes focus on 5 randomly generated questions based off of a single music artist's collection of tracks.

Audio Quiz

Listen to the song preview and choose the name of the song from 4 options.

Lyric Quiz

_COMING_SOON_ View a portion of a song's lyrics and choose the name of the song from 4 options.

Data

Spotify provides a considerable amount of data through an exposed API. KnowSong connects to Spotify from a dedicated servlet for all of the heavy requests and extra security. KnowSong utilizes a relational database to store data on artists as users make requests for new games. Subsequent games using that artist are built from the data on hand instead of using up limited requests to Spotify's API.
