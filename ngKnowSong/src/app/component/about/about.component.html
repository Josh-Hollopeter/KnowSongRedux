KnowSong provides a way for people to test their knowledge of music artists. Connect a Spotify account and quickly start a trivia game! Our game modes focus on 5 randomly generated questions based off of a single music artist's collection of tracks.

NOTE: Free spotify acounts may not have access to certain artists. If an artist does not finish loading and returns you to home page. That artist is unavailable. Sorry! Activate a premium subscription to Spotify for full access!
